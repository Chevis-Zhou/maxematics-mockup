Giving back

The Gen. H. Norman Schwarzkopf Scholarship

Max's grandfather spent his life in service — to his country, and to the young men and women who served alongside him. What fewer people know is that he was also an educator, teaching calculus and mechanics at West Point. This scholarship exists in his memory, continuing his belief in the importance of education.

A full scholastic year of tutoring is awarded to one student each year, in late August.

"You can't help someone else up a hill without getting a little closer to the top yourself."

— Gen. H. Norman Schwarzkopf
