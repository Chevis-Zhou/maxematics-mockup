About Max

The experts in anything were once beginners.

Max Schwarzkopf is a senior at Tampa Preparatory School pursuing a Biomedical STEM concentration — captain of the varsity rowing team, director of the school's Writing Center, and founder of the Pie & Pi Club. Since 2022 he has also tutored middle, upper and home-schooled students across math, science and language.

"If you think of something as 'hard' it absolutely will be — but it doesn't need to be that way. Academic success is the intersection of preparation and attitude. I tackle concepts one problem at a time and hope to inspire a shift in thinking that makes learning fun."

— Max Schwarzkopf

Giving back

The General H. Norman Schwarzkopf Scholarship

Gen. Schwarzkopf spent his life in service — and, less famously, in education, teaching calculus and mechanics at West Point. In his memory, Maxematics awards a full scholastic year of tutoring to one student annually, in late August.
